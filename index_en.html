LBO Company

Local Biomass Opportunities also known as L.B.O; is a French company specialized in identifying and mastering the development of innovative projects with high added value in the field of renewable energy.

Coordinating a range of partners, L.B.O draws and harmonises the implementation of new fields of production/distribution of energy efficient and eco-friendly products, eco-environment (fuel and electricity). Consequently, L.B.O, conscious of its social responsibility, offers durable and sustainable solutions designed to meet the local energy needs of growing populations.

ECB Project

L.B.O’s ECB Project objective is to implement and operate a unit demonstrating the possibilities of production of cellulosic ethanol in Brittany, largest biomass producer.

Cellulosic ethanol is an alcohol for industrial use (agro-fuel) produced from non-food resources. Its energy efficiency and effectiveness (cost of production/energy power) positions’ it as a serious competitor of other energy products from agricultural transformations.

The main objective of this ECB Project is to demonstrate the technical and economic feasibility of producing, from local resources, ethanol intended for local use, resulting in energy and money savings.

MGO Project - Senegal

Our integrated solution is a tool for social and economic development in the medium term appropriate to the local/regional scale.

Our goal is the production - distribution of 95.6% ethanol and electricity short circuits closer to local consumption (Primarily power unit engines, industries and later vehicles).

Through the development of ecological agricultural production of recognized lignocellulosic biomass, the Miscanthus which allows a diversification of farming activity, creates value and additional compensations to traditional agricultural productions.

We transform the biomass into 95.6% pure bioethanol in a micro-bio refinery, a system currently used on other continents.
